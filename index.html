HYDROWAVE

A highschool team fighting for a green and sustainable future!

We are a Slovenian highschool group of five students from GVÅ  BIC Ljubljana.

We are a young and newly formed team, we got together in 2024 to compete on a internacional turnament for a better and greener future.

Our projects are mostly focus on hydrogen and its potential in the future.

We are not afraid of chalanges and we like exploring and learning new things.

On this website you will finde all about our past, ongoing and future projects, you will also finde some information about the group and its members.

Soon enough you will be able to buy our game "HydroWave-The Game" on this website.

We wish you an enjoyable journey on our website.

Our team hopes you discover new knowledge and hopes that you and your family finde joy playing our game.
